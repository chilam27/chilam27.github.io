    PUBG Player Role Analysis

    Grouping professional players in Player Unknown’s Battlegrounds (PUBG) PCS Charity Showdown tournament into different groups of players using the k-means model. The tournament data will be scraped by selenium on the https://esports.pubgrank.org/ page. Building a simple regression model to analyze the relationship between the players from different clusters in each team and the team’s overall placement in the tournament.
